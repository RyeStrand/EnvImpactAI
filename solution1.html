POSSIBLE SOLUTION #1

Shift to Renewable Energy Sources

One of the most impactful solutions to this issue is powering data centers with renewable energy such as solar, wind, or hydroelectric power. Many data centers rely on traditional fossil fuels, leading to high carbon emissions. By transitioning to cleaner energy sources, AI companies can significantly reduce the carbon footprint of training and running large AI models
Renewable energy sources are carbon-neutral or carbon-negative, meaning they do not contribute to the emissions that cause global warming. For instance, wind and solar power produce electricity without releasing CO2, making them perfect for offsetting the carbon-heavy demands of AI models. (8)
Google and Microsoft are leading examples of companies powering their data centers with 100% renewable energy. (7)
Shifting to renewable energy not only addresses the environmental issues specific to AI but also plays a significant role in the broader global effort for sustainability.
