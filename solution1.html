POSSIBLE SOLUTION #1

Shift to Renewable Energy Sources

Prioritize energy-efficient models, optimize model training to reduce carbon emissions, and shift toward more sustainable AI development practices. Tech companies should also invest in renewable energy sources to power their data centers.
